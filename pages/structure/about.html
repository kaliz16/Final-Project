About Me

Mother, Friend & Photographer

Hi, I'm Susy. A photographer based in Nashville, TN. I'm self-taught with a sincere passion for all things photographic, beautiful light, fresh food, sincere people, and beautiful spaces. I love keeping things simple in life and on set as I find it always produces the best results with the least stress. No matter the size of the project I always bring the same passion and dedication to each job, and it's my priority to make sure everyone goes home happy! Let's connect and make awesome work together.
